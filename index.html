❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum down payment in Malaysia? ▼

For first-time homebuyers, the minimum down payment is 10%. For second and subsequent properties, it increases to 30% (or higher depending on Bank Negara Malaysia regulations).

How is DSR (Debt Service Ratio) calculated? ▼

DSR = (Total Monthly Debt Commitments / Gross Monthly Income) × 100%. Banks typically approve loans with DSR below 60-70%, depending on your income level.

What documents are needed for a home loan? ▼

IC/Passport, 3-6 months payslips, EPF statements, bank statements, employment confirmation letter, and property booking receipt.
